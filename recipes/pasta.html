My Pasta Recipe

Description

This is a recipe of how to make my favorite and highly nutritious pasta, follow along for details

Ingredients

  • 1(16 ounce) package fusili (spiral) pasta
  • 3 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese, cubed
  • 1/2 pound salami, cubed
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni, cut in half
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cut into 1 inch pieces
  • 1 (10 ounce) can black olives, drained
  • 1 (4 ounce) jar pimentoes, drained
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle italian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Gather all the Ingredients
  2. Get a large pot
  3. Combine Pasta with tomatoes
  4. Enjoy
