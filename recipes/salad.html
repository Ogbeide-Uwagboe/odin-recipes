Dad's Creamy Cucumber Salad

Description

Best Cucumber salad you could ever taste made by my Dad. You can learn how to make it here

Ingredients

  • 2 large Cucumbers, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 sweet onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon sea salt

Dressing

  • 1 1/2 cups mayonnaise, or more to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon drilled dill weed
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Mix Cucumber, onion and salt together in a bowl
  2. Turn Cucumber mixture into a colander set over a bowl in a sink
  3. Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, dill in a bowl
  4. Pour over Cucumber mixture and stir until coated
  5. cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours before serving
  6. Enjoy
